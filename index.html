About Me

I am a Graphic Designer primarily focusing on moving image with a passion for digital art and a strong focus on typographic approaches in static and kinetic design, I aim to constantly push myself out of my comfort zone and explore what the future of graphic arts and design might look like.

As an Artist I’m always keen to learn something new and explore new facets of design to improve my skills even further. During my time at University I’ve enjoyed working with like minded creatives as part of a team whilst gaining insight and knowledge across Editorial Design, Digital Design and Motion Design.
